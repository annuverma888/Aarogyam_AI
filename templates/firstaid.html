🩹 First Aid Guide

Learn life-saving first aid procedures for common medical emergencies.

❤️ CPR

  • Call emergency services immediately.
  • Place both hands in the center of the chest.
  • Push hard and fast (100–120 compressions/min).
  • Continue until medical help arrives.

🩸 Bleeding

  • Apply firm pressure with a clean cloth.
  • Raise the injured area if possible.
  • Do not remove embedded objects.
  • Visit the nearest hospital immediately.

🔥 Burns

  • Cool the burn under running water.
  • Do not apply toothpaste or ice.
  • Cover with a clean dressing.
  • Seek medical care if severe.

🦴 Fracture

  • Keep the injured limb still.
  • Apply a splint if available.
  • Use an ice pack to reduce swelling.
  • Go to the nearest hospital.

😮 Choking

  • Encourage the person to cough.
  • Give 5 back blows.
  • Perform abdominal thrusts if needed.
  • Call emergency services.

🐍 Snake Bite

  • Keep the victim calm.
  • Immobilize the affected limb.
  • Do not cut or suck the wound.
  • Reach the nearest hospital immediately.

⚡ Electric Shock

  • Turn off the power source first.
  • Do not touch the victim directly.
  • Call emergency medical services.
  • Start CPR if required.

💔 Heart Attack

  • Call an ambulance immediately.
  • Keep the patient calm.
  • Loosen tight clothing.
  • Do not let the patient walk.
⚠ Disclaimer: This guide is for educational purposes only and does not replace professional medical advice.
